The Concept

Built for owners who build
real businesses.

Most brokerage owners understand that title is a significant revenue stream — and that it's leaving their office on every transaction. The question has always been how to capture it without building an entirely new operation. We solved that problem.

What We Bring
The Inspire Group and Federal Guardian Title operate the complete title platform — underwriter relationship, compliance framework, staffing, licensing, and back-office. The infrastructure is already built. You attach to it.
What You Bring
Your brand. Your brokerage. A culture where agents understand the value of the affiliated title entity and choose to use it. You don't run operations. You don't manage staff. You lead your office — and the entity earns.
The Structure

Three tiers.
One clean platform.

Platform
IL Master LLC
The Inspire Group and Federal Guardian Title. Owns and operates the platform. Manages all underwriting, compliance, staffing, and back-office across every JV entity.
JV Entity
Your Brokerage Title Co.
A dedicated, licensed Illinois title entity carrying your brand. You hold 49% Class A equity. IL Master holds 51% and runs day-to-day operations. Distributions flow to equity holders on a periodic basis.
Agent Layer
Class B Equity Pool
Your top agents may purchase a 1% equity position in the entity for a nominal one-time cost. Participation is entirely voluntary — agents who own a stake make informed choices about where they close.
Ownership at a Glance

The equity split is fixed at formation and does not change with volume, B share activity, or time. This is ownership — not a revenue share arrangement.

The Agent Equity Pool

Turn your best agents into
invested owners.

The Class B structure is an optional layer inside each JV entity. It's one of the more elegant tools in the platform — simple to explain, powerful in practice.

$99
One-Time Entry
Each Class B position costs an agent $99. Once. No recurring fees, no ongoing commitment. A nominal stake that creates a real ownership mindset.
1%
Per Position
Each agent holds exactly 1% of the Class B pool. Up to 20 positions available per entity. Distributions are quarterly and based on net entity performance — not per-transaction payments.
100%
Unallocated to You
Any unsold Class B position flows entirely to you, the JV Partner — not back into the shared pool with IL Master. Every unsold share adds directly to your quarterly distribution.
Why This Works

Built around ownership,
not obligation.

No operational burden
You hold equity. We run the company. There is no staffing, no licensing management, no compliance monitoring on your end. The platform exists so you don't have to build one.
Your brand, your entity
This isn't a referral to someone else's title company. It's your company — with your name on it — operating inside a proven platform infrastructure. That distinction matters to your agents and your clients.
Periodic distributions, not per-closing fees
Distributions are based on net entity revenue and paid quarterly. This is an equity ownership arrangement — structured and compliant — not a transaction-based referral program.
Designed to scale
Title is the foundation. The platform is built to extend to mortgage and additional ancillary profit centers as the partnership matures. This is a platform, not a product.
